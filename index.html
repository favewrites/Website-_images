Our Story

Baked with Love,
Styled with Grace

Bloomberry Café was born from a simple belief: that every pastry should feel like a gift. Founded in 2020, we blend French patisserie tradition with modern artistry, creating desserts that delight the eyes as much as the palate.

Our ingredients are sourced from sustainable farms, our flour is stone-milled, and every creation is handcrafted by chefs who see baking as a form of love. From our signature cupcakes to our ceremonial matcha lattes, we invite you to experience luxury in its sweetest form.
